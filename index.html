Bhaav-2-Bhaav

Did you ever imagine that real-time prices of crops, fresh fruits, vegetables, and essential agricultural products could be tracked and updated at your fingertips? Bhaav-2-Bhaav, India’s first comprehensive online market price tracker for agricultural goods, provides farmers and traders with the latest and most accurate market price updates. We aggregate data from more than 1000 markets, delivering real-time price information to over a million farmers and stakeholders across the nation. Whether it’s wholesale markets, local mandis, or government updates, Bhaav-2-Bhaav has everything you need to stay updated on agricultural prices.

Gone are the days of manual tracking and delayed information. With Bhaav-2-Bhaav, you can now check the market prices of various crops and commodities online, ensuring you make the best decisions without travelling long distances or relying on outdated reports.

  • Real-Time Alerts: Get notified instantly when the price of your selected crop changes. This helps you decide the best time to sell or purchase commodities.
  • Historical Trends: View price trends over weeks, months, or even years, allowing farmers to anticipate market fluctuations and plan accordingly.
  • Localized Data: Receive data specific to your region or market, from wholesale mandis to nearby local markets.

Farmers First – Direct from Markets

Bhaav-2-Bhaav is closely associated with local markets and farmers across the country to ensure the freshest and most reliable data. Our platform helps farmers get better deals and make informed decisions based on accurate and real-time information.

At Bhaav-2-Bhaav, we believe in empowering farmers with direct access to market prices. Our platform gathers information from a vast network of sources and delivers it straight to your mobile or desktop. Whether you need the latest prices of crops or want to track historical trends, Bhaav-2-Bhaav ensures you are always informed.

Best quality data for our quality-conscious farmers.

We are proud to be associated closely with the farmers from whom we source our real-time market prices. Most of our data is sourced directly from markets and verified sources, which not only ensures the best prices and accurate updates for our customers but also helps the farmers stay informed with the latest market conditions.

Hassle-Free Access & Price Alerts

Whether you are checking market prices or setting price alerts, Bhaav-2-Bhaav makes it easy to track prices with a user-friendly interface. Farmers and traders can use our platform to ensure they get the best market rates and make timely decisions.

Our goal is to make the daily lives of farmers easier by providing the tools they need to succeed in an ever-changing market. With features like price alerts and historical trends, Bhaav-2-Bhaav is set to revolutionize how India’s agricultural community interacts with the market.
